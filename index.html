ISRAEL MATURANO

ARTIST · PROGRAMMER · DESIGNER

ABOUT ME

My name is Israel Maturano and I am a second year at Virginia Tech majoring in computer science and studio art. I do many things, I like to say. I am very passionate about design in all sorts of areas. Out of the twenty years of life I have expierenced, fourteen were spent developing technical skills. However, I have always had a desire to create. I can still remember one of my first drawings in kindergarten. Picture it, an ice skater dancing across the ice. Of course, it was illustrated in crayon on printer paper. Some would say I peaked early.

Looking forward, I hope to continue refining my skills as an artist and software engineer. Along the way, I would like to discover ways to merge these two parts of my identity. Using code to create art. There is a lot I have yet to learn, which is both exciting and terrifying. Regardless, I move forward! I welcome you to join me through this journey, even if your stay is just momentary.
